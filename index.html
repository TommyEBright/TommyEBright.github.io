About

Research, teaching,
and applied practice.

My research focuses on force platform methodology and strength and conditioning — particularly countermovement jump force-time analysis and accentuated eccentric loading strategies in youth and adult athletes.

I lead biomechanics teaching from Level 4 through to Masters level, with a strong emphasis on applied, qualitative understanding of human movement. From September 2026 I take on the role of MSc Sports Performance Science Programme Coordinator.
