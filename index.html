About

What this site is for.

This site exists primarily as a teaching and learning resource for biomechanics — covering everything from foundational mechanics at undergraduate level through to advanced force platform methodology at Masters. You'll find interactive tools, level-specific content, and resources built around real sport science practice.

Alongside the teaching content, the site shares ongoing research projects — including student-contributor work — and the latest publications and outputs.

I'm Dr. Tommy Bright — I hold a PhD in Sports Biomechanics and have extensive applied and research experience in biomechanics and strength and conditioning across university, academy, and elite sport settings.
