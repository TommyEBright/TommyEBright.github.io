Themes

Accentuated Eccentric Loading

Mechanisms, prescription, and practitioner application of AEL strategies across youth and adult athletes. Includes survey work, narrative reviews, and experimental designs.

Primary

Force Plate Methodology

CMJ force-time analysis, event detection, numerical integration, and reliability of force-derived metrics using Kistler and VALD systems.

Applied

Athletic Performance

Golf performance science, youth athletic development, LTAD frameworks, and applied S&C in academy and elite sport settings.
