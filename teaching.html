Biomechanics

Biomechanics resources.

Good biomechanics teaching starts with observation, not numbers. Drawing on Duane Knudson's qualitative analysis framework, students develop the ability to watch a movement and ask the right questions before they reach for a force platform.

Resources are designed to build understanding progressively across all levels.

"The purpose of qualitative analysis is to improve performance through systematic observation and evaluation."
Duane Knudson — Fundamentals of Biomechanics
