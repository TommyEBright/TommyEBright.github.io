About DFA-alpha1

Detrended Fluctuation Analysis (DFA) alpha1 quantifies the fractal scaling of RR interval fluctuations. As exercise intensity increases, parasympathetic withdrawal causes alpha1 to decline:

alpha1 ≈ 0.75 → Aerobic threshold (LT1)
alpha1 ≈ 0.50 → Anaerobic threshold (LT2)

Rogers & Gronwald (2022) · Gronwald et al. (2020) · Altini & Plews (2021)

Breath-by-Breath / Averaged Exercise Data

Enter exercise data from a metabolic cart (e.g. COSMED, VYNTUS, Cortex). Rows can be individual breaths or averaged epochs. Minimum ~8 rows required. V̇CO₂ is required for V-slope VT1 detection; V̇E enables V̇E/V̇CO₂ nadir VT2 detection.
