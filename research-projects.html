Student Research

Active projects.

A student-contributor research initiative bringing students into real research as genuine contributors — not observers. Students collect data, run analyses, and co-author outputs.

Strand 1 · Active

CMJ Waveform Analysis in Academy Football

Force-time waveform data from Forest, Coventry City, and Plymouth academy players. Exploring maturation, academy category, and longitudinal development.

CMJFootballVALD
Strand 2 · Active

AEL Load-Response in Youth Athletes

Acute and chronic responses to accentuated eccentric loading protocols using a MATLAB force platform analysis pipeline.

AELYouthMATLAB
Strand 3 · Developing

Match-Day Fatigue Monitoring

Using CMJ force-time metrics as a neuromuscular fatigue indicator around competitive match play, tracked longitudinally across an academy season.

MonitoringFatigueMatch-Day
